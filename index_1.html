Photography Portfolio

How I got into Photography..

I discovered my passion for photography during a family trip to the mountains. Armed with a smartphone camera, I began capturing the breathtaking landscapes and intricate details of nature. The process of framing a shot and waiting for the perfect moment felt exhilarating. As I shared my photos with friends and family, their positive feedback fueled my enthusiasm. What started as a casual hobby quickly grew into a deep-seated passion, driving me to explore and document the beauty around me through the lens.

Present Day..

Despite the demands of college and other responsibilities, I continue to pursue my passion for photography. Balancing academics, extracurricular activities, and personal commitments requires careful time management, but I always carve out moments for my creative outlet. Weekends become opportunities for mini photo excursions. I've also joined the college photography club, where I collaborate with like-minded individuals, participate in workshops, and gain new insights. This pursuit not only provides a necessary escape but also enriches my perspective, making me more attuned to the beauty in everyday moments.
